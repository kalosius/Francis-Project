About the School

Welcome to ST.Francis Senior
Secondary School Busunju

Established in 1909, ST.Francis Senior Secondary School stands as a testament to the visionary collaboration between the Buganda Kingdom and the Church Missionary Society. Our foundation was laid by pioneering missionaries and devoted community leaders. For over a century, our institution has been a beacon of academic excellence, spiritual growth, and character development in Uganda.

What began as a boys' school has evolved through numerous historical milestones to become a co-educational institution of repute. We maintain our strong Christian values while delivering top-tier education.

The Famous Bowers Pillar

The Iconic Bowers Pillar

The Bowers Pillar stands as a testament to our school's rich history and enduring legacy. Named after our founding headmaster, it has witnessed generations of students passing through its shadow for nearly a century.

Did You Know?

  • Built in 1925, the pillar is over 95 years old
  • Made from locally sourced red brick
  • Bears the school motto in gold lettering
